SHORT-TERM ACCOMMODATION AGREEMENT

This Short-Term Accommodation Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between The Host and The Guest in relation to The Reservation.

1. Booking and Stay Period

This Agreement applies to the accommodation booked by The Guest from The Host for the dates specified in The Reservation ("Stay Period").

The Guest acknowledges that this is a short-term accommodation arrangement only and does not create a tenancy, lease, or any other right of occupation beyond the Stay Period.

2. Acceptance of Agreement

The Guest is deemed to have read, understood, accepted, and agreed to be bound by the terms of this Agreement upon payment of any part or all of the accommodation charges, whether the booking is made through Airbnb, Booking.com, another booking platform, or directly with The Host.

3. Use of the Property

The Guest agrees to:

a. Treat the Property, its furnishings, fixtures, fittings, equipment, and contents with care and respect;

b. Keep the Property in a clean and tidy condition throughout the Stay Period;

c. Use the Property only for lawful residential accommodation purposes;

d. Ensure that all occupants, visitors, and invitees comply with the terms of this Agreement; and

e. Comply with all House Rules provided by The Host, whether communicated through the booking platform, electronically, in writing, or displayed at the Property.

4. Damage and Liability

The Guest shall be responsible for any loss, damage, breakage, theft, excessive cleaning requirements, missing items, or other costs arising from the acts or omissions of The Guest, any member of The Guest's party, or any visitor invited to the Property during the Stay Period.

The Guest agrees to reimburse The Host for the reasonable cost of repairing, replacing, cleaning, or otherwise remedying any such loss or damage.

The Host reserves the right to recover such costs through the applicable booking platform, security deposit, payment method, or any other lawful means available.

5. House Rules

The Guest agrees to comply with all House Rules applicable to the Property.

Failure to comply with House Rules may constitute a breach of this Agreement and may result in additional charges, termination of the booking, removal from the Property, or other remedies available to The Host, subject to applicable law.

6. Noise and Respect for Neighbours

The Guest shall conduct themselves in a manner that does not unreasonably disturb neighbours, nearby residents, or other occupants.

The Guest agrees to keep noise to a reasonable level at all times and to observe any quiet hours specified in the House Rules or otherwise communicated by The Host.

Parties, events, gatherings, excessive noise, amplified music, or any activity likely to cause disturbance, complaints, or disruption are strictly prohibited unless expressly authorised in writing by The Host.

7. Nuisance and Illegal Activities

The Guest shall not engage in, permit, or facilitate any activity at the Property that may constitute a nuisance, annoyance, hazard, disturbance, or inconvenience to neighbours, visitors, or the local community.

The Guest shall not engage in any unlawful, antisocial, offensive, dangerous, or disruptive conduct at the Property.

The Host reserves the right to take appropriate action if complaints are received regarding the conduct of The Guest or members of The Guest's party.

8. No Smoking

Smoking, vaping, or the use of any tobacco, nicotine, cannabis, or similar products is strictly prohibited inside the Property unless expressly permitted by The Host in writing.

Where smoking is permitted in designated outdoor areas, The Guest shall dispose of all smoking materials safely and responsibly and shall ensure that smoke does not enter the Property or cause nuisance to others.

The Guest shall be liable for any additional cleaning, deodorising, repair, redecorating, fire alarm call-out charges, or other costs incurred as a result of a breach of this clause.

Any evidence of smoking within the Property may result in additional charges being applied to cover remediation costs.

9. Maximum Occupancy

The maximum number of persons permitted to occupy the Property at any time shall be the occupancy limit specified in The Reservation, listing description, or House Rules.

The Guest shall not permit occupancy of the Property by any person in excess of the stated maximum occupancy limit without the prior written consent of The Host.

For the purposes of this clause, occupancy includes overnight stays and any period during which a person is present at the Property for an extended duration.

The Host reserves the right to charge additional fees, refuse entry to additional persons, or terminate the booking where the maximum occupancy limit is exceeded.

Any breach of this clause shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement.

10. Unauthorised Visitors and Guests

Only those persons included in The Reservation, or otherwise approved by The Host, may stay overnight at the Property.

The Guest shall not permit visitors, invitees, contractors, or other third parties to enter or use the Property in a manner that exceeds reasonable residential use or creates a nuisance, disturbance, safety risk, or breach of this Agreement.

The Property shall not be used for parties, events, commercial activities, filming, photography sessions, receptions, gatherings, or functions without the prior written consent of The Host.

The Guest shall be responsible and liable for the conduct of all visitors and invitees and for any loss, damage, expense, or liability arising from their presence at the Property.

The Host may require any unauthorised person to leave the Property immediately and may terminate The Reservation without refund, subject to applicable law, where there has been a breach of this clause.

Any breach of this clause shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement.

11. Security, Keys and Access Devices

The Guest shall take reasonable care to secure the Property at all times during the Stay Period, including ensuring that all doors, windows, gates, alarms, locks, key safes, and other security devices are properly secured when the Property is unattended.

The Guest shall be responsible for the safekeeping of all keys, access cards, fobs, parking permits, remote controls, codes, and other access devices provided by The Host.

The Guest shall not copy, share, distribute, or permit the unauthorised use of any key, access code, access device, or security information relating to the Property.

The Guest shall immediately notify The Host of any lost, stolen, damaged, or malfunctioning key, access device, security code, or lock.

The Guest shall be liable for all reasonable costs incurred by The Host as a result of lost, stolen, damaged, unreturned, or misused keys or access devices, including but not limited to locksmith charges, replacement keys, replacement locks, reprogramming of electronic access systems, replacement fobs, security call-out fees, and associated administrative costs.

Where The Guest, any member of The Guest's party, or any visitor requires assistance to gain access to the Property due to loss of keys, forgotten access codes, lockouts, or misuse of access devices, The Host may charge a reasonable call-out fee together with any third-party costs incurred.

The Guest shall not disclose access codes, key safe codes, alarm codes, Wi-Fi credentials, or other security information to any person not authorised to occupy the Property.

Any failure to secure the Property or any misuse of keys, access devices, or security systems that results in loss, damage, theft, security risk, or expense shall constitute a breach of this Agreement, and The Guest shall be responsible for all resulting losses, costs, and damages.

13. Material Breach

A material breach of the House Rules, Damage and Liability, Noise and Respect for Neighbours, Nuisance and Illegal Activities, or No Smoking provisions of this Agreement shall entitle The Host, subject to applicable law, to require The Guest and all occupants to vacate the Property immediately.

Where a booking is terminated for material breach, no refund shall be payable except where required by applicable law or the terms of the booking platform through which the reservation was made.

The Guest shall remain liable for any loss, damage, costs, expenses, compensation claims, penalties, or additional charges arising from such breach.

14. Cancellation and Refunds

Any cancellation, refund, credit, or adjustment of accommodation charges shall be governed by The Host's cancellation and refund policy in effect at the time The Reservation is made.

The Guest acknowledges that any entitlement to a refund is subject to that policy and any applicable terms of the booking platform through which the reservation was made.

15. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, The Host shall not be liable for loss of money, valuables, personal belongings, vehicles, or other property belonging to The Guest or any member of The Guest's party, except where such loss results from The Host's negligence or liability cannot be excluded by law.

The Guest is responsible for securing their personal property during the Stay Period.

16. General

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Property is located.

By making payment of part or all of the accommodation charges relating to The Reservation, The Guest acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Agreement.
